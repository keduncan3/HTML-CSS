Hi there, my name is Kendall Duncan and I am a graphic design student at Western Carolina University in Cullowhee, North Carolina.

 

I have a passion for design that pushes me to always try and learn something new. It makes me smile to tell people that "I love what I do", and in the end I hope my work shows that. In addition to my education in the graphic design realm, I have extensive knowledge in photography. This year will mark my third year as a photography student.

 

 

Interests and Fun Facts

I play golf year round and have been playing since I was a little kid. I am very outdoorsy and love going on hiking and backpacking trips. I just recently made plans to thru-hike a portion of the Appalachian Trail. Photography is a huge passion of mine and I carry my DSLR almost everywhere I go, you could call it an extension of my body. I spend a lot of my down time on educational sites such as stacksocial.com learning new tips and tricks for web and graphic design. Youtube is almost a necessity when learning new things.

 

 

About this Site

KEDStudios.com is a custom portfolio website I designed in Adobe Muse. Adobe Muse is, in my mind, the most foremost program for web development. It may not be able to replace DreamWeaver completely yet, but it is well on that track. This site uses valid CSS which conforms to W3C's Web Standards.

 

This site was built and is being maintained on my 17" Toshiba Satellite.

 

Hosting of this site is on a NameCheap server that allows students and educators cheap and affordable hosting.
